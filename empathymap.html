Our Interview Findings

After conducting interviews, the conclusion was reached that the target customer for this product fits the following criteria: Aware that they use their phone more than they should. Wants to reduce their phone usage. Has the ability to leave their phone inaccessible to normal use for some time. Constant mobile phone availability for some people equals constant distraction, but the need to communicate in an emergency provides a valid excuse not to simply leave the phone behind.
